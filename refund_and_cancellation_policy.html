Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for products or services purchased through the Platform drsanchitagera.github.io.

  • Cancellations: Requests must be made within 7 dats of placing the order. Cancellation may not be possible if the product has already been shipped or is out for delivery. In such cases, you may reject the product at the doorstep.
  • Perishable Items: Cancellation requests for perishable items (e.g., flowers, eatables) are not accepted. Refunds or replacements may be provided if the delivered product quality is proven unsatisfactory.
  • Damaged or Defective Products: If you receive a damaged or defective item, report it to our customer service team within 7 dats of receipt. The seller/merchant will verify the issue before processing a replacement or refund.
  • Product Mismatch: If the product received does not match the description or your expectations, notify customer service within 7 dats. After review, an appropriate decision will be made.
  • Manufacturer Warranty: For products covered under manufacturer warranty, please contact the manufacturer directly for resolution.
  • Refund Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 7 dats.
